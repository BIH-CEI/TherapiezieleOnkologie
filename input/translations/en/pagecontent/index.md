### Therapy Goals in Oncology

This Implementation Guide defines FHIR profiles for documenting **oncological therapy goals** (German: *Therapieziele*) — the goals patients and clinicians agree on when planning cancer treatment (e.g. curative intent, life prolongation, symptom control, quality of life).

It is part of the BIH-CEI / Gematik Onkologie collaboration and is published in German with an English translation.
